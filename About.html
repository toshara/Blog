    About

    The Motivation Core

    TheMotivationCore was founded by Ms. Toshara Gopaul.

    Ms. Toshara Gopaul, has gone through a lengthy journey to self love and self confidence. For many years she strived her hardest to tap into the potential within her that she felt was hidden away. After finally attaining all her life goals that made her into a better individual with a strong mind, heart and soul, she felt it was only right that she shared her experience and knowledge that she would have gained, so that she can assist those who are also struggling to find their inner power.

    It is through immense and genuine self love and strength that we are able to achieve the impossible and be healthy and happy. TheMotivationCore aims to assist everyone regardless of their race, age, religion or gender identity, to attain a version of themselvess, that they didn't even know existed. The best version of them so that they made lead the happiest lives possible.
